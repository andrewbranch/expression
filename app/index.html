What?

My name is Andrew. I’m a web designer and developer, a musician, and, at the time of this writing, an electrical engineering student at Auburn University. I’m currently working on a design project in which I’m making some very cool additions to the electronics of my acoustic guitar. As I make progress, I’ll add media and documentation here. You’ll find video and audio clips, photos and figures, and moderately technical exposition, as well as links to the more formal documentation that the course requires.

Background

I am very lucky to have a beautiful 2010 Taylor 614ce. At that time, the 400 series and everything above shipped with a magnetic pickup and preamplification system, christened the Taylor Expression System® (ES). Taylor made it out to be a really big deal, claiming incredible improvements in tone and natural acoustical character over the ubiquitous piezoelectric under-saddle transducer (UST).
